LinguPhones

"Breaking barriers and connecting worlds with Linguphones".

What are the earphones?

LinguPhones are head- phones that allow people to communicate fluently and practically, regardless of their language.

LinguPhones prioritizes global and inclusive communication, enabling that with real-time multiple language translation, an AI assistant and specialized volume control for hearing disabilities, improving daily tasks and fostering connection through technology!

Design and Funtionalities

Made with polymer and some parts with gel.

Has the capability to translate 60 languages.

It is equipped with an artificial intelligence assistant.

An application is available to adjust the volume for the hearing impaired.

Price

Currently the LinguPhones are not available. LinguPhones will be launched at the end of August this year, together with the LinguPhones application.

LinguPhones will be available at an affordable price of $199.99.
